 OpenAI Custom GPTs – Finance AI Assistants

This section showcases custom-built GPTs designed to solve real-world finance and ERP use cases. Each assistant focuses on a specific domain within Oracle Finance, combining accounting knowledge with AI-driven guidance.

---

 🔹 1. Finance & O2C ERP Mentor
An AI assistant focused on the *Order-to-Cash (O2C)* cycle in ERP systems.

Key Features:
- Explains end-to-end O2C lifecycle (Order → Billing → Receivables → Collections)
- Covers accounting fundamentals, GST, and revenue recognition
- Simplifies complex ERP concepts for beginners and intermediate users

Use Cases:
- Understanding O2C process flow
- Learning accounting entries for AR transactions
- Interview preparation for finance roles
